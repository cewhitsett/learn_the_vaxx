Meet the Squad

We are a group of past and present Harvey Mudd students who devised this project as a part of the Hack 4 Black Lives hackathon put on by students at the Claremont colleges. It was an event to:

"challenge to practice active anti-racism by focusing your skills and passions on developing a solution that supports Black Communities and battles the oppression that they face."
In the spirit of this challenge, this team compiled information to combat common misconceptions and distrust towards the COVID-19 vaccine in the Black community. This is important to the team because it is disproportionately affecting communities of color, and a higher vaccination rate will help mitigate the damage.

Thank you for supporting our project, and we encourage you to share this!
